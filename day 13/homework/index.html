whiplash is a psychological drama/indie film that came out in 2014.
it is directed and written by Damien Chazelle.
A promising young drummer enrolls at a cut-throat music conservatory where his dreams
of greatness are mentored by an instructor who will stop at nothing to realize a student's potential.
Driven by his demanding music teacher, drummer Andrew is determined to succeed as a jazz
musician — even if it destroys his personality.
